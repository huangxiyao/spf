Logging in with e-mail address and password

Enter your "primary" e-mail address, which is commonly your name (firstname.lastname) followed by "@hp.com". For example:

john.doe@hp.com
sally.smith@hp.com

If your email address is not accepted, check PeopleFinder for your true "primary" email address in the "E-mail" field in PeopleFinder.

The Password is the password you use to log your PC onto the HP network. If you cannot authenticate your e-mail account or have forgotten your password, contact your local helpdesk.
